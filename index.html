Company History

Interactive Radiant International Academy was founded in 2021 with a mission to equip the next generation with the necessary skills to thrive in the digital era. Our focus is on coding, STEM education, and research, recognizing the crucial role these areas play in today's world. We believe in making quality education accessible to all, fostering curiosity, and encouraging exploration in every student. Our experienced tutors are committed to guiding each student on their individual learning journey, helping them unlock their potential and prepare for the future.

Our Mission

At Interactive Radiant International Academy, our mission is to inspire and empower students through enriching, engaging, and accessible coding and STEM education. We aim to nurture curiosity, foster a love for learning, and equip our students with the skills they need to succeed in a technology-driven future.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading online tutoring platform, known for excellence in coding and STEM education. We envision a world where every student, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to explore, learn, and excel in these critical fields. We aspire to cultivate a community of lifelong learners, ready to make a significant contribution to the digital world.
